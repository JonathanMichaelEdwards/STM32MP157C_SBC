R_Tot = R_Set + R_Term                    
R_Tot < V_OV / (100*i_Set)              

R_Set  = (V_TH * R_Tot) / V_OV 
R_Term = R_Tot - R_Set    
i_Set  > V_OV / (100 * R_Tot)

--- Setting values
V_OV   = 14V     - Overvoltage
V_TH   = 1.2V    - Threshold Voltage
i_Set ~= 1uA   - Set Current
